    \item Solve the following system of matrix equations
      $$
        2X-3Y=\begin{pmatrix}

          4 & -5

      $$
      Hint: The answer is 
      $$
        X=\dfrac{1}{7}\begin{pmatrix}
          -11 & -13 \\
          26 & -29
        \end{pmatrix}, ~~~~ Y=\dfrac{1}{7}\begin{pmatrix}
          2 & -4 \\
          1 & -3
        \end{pmatrix}
      $$

            \end{cases} \Longrightarrow \begin{cases}
              2X-3Y=\begin{pmatrix}

                2 & 6 \\

            \end{cases}
            \\
            \\
            \\
          $

          \\
          $
